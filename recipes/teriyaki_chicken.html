Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki Chicken is a quintessential Japanese dish featuring juicy, tender chicken glazed in a glossy, sweet-and-savory sauce. The chicken is traditionally pan-seared until the skin is crisp and golden, then simmered directly in a reduced glaze made from soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar until the sauce thickens and tightly coats the meat

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs - 4 large pieces
  • Oil 1 tablespoon
  • Soy sauce 1/4 cup
  • Mirin 1/4 cup
  • Sake 1/4 cup (Japanese cooking sake)
  • Sugar 1-2 tablespoon
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar in a small bowl until the sugar dissolves
  2. Sear the chicken skin side until crisp
  3. Cook for 5-6 min until golden brown
  4. Pour the sauce mixture directly into the hot pan with the chicken
  5. Bring to a boil then lwoer heat to medium, simmer for 2-3 minutes
  6. Spoon the liquid over the chicken continously until the sauce becomes thick glossy
  7. Remove chicken from the pan, let it rest for 2 mins
  8. slice into thick strips
  9. Plate over rice
  10. Pour the remaining sticky glaze from the pan over the top
  11. Finish with some sesame seeds and green onions
